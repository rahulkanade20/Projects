Product Comparator


About Product Comparator -

Product Comparator is a tool developed in R which makes use of YouTube comments, amazon reviews and amazon ratings to make a quick comparison between two products like 2 mobiles, Television sets etc. to check which product amongst the two is more popular


Tech Stack Used -

R
Web Scraping
Syuzhet(R library)
