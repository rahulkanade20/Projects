Multisource Risk and Compliance Detection Software (MRCDS) -

In an organization there is always a possibility/risk of sensitive information being shared with other organizations knowingly or unknowingly. This can put an organization at risk from a compliance or business standpoint. Same is true for an individual sharing his or her private details at the wrong place knowingly or unknowingly which can put the individual in jeopardy. Multisource risk and compliance detection software is a software-based solution which will parse text from various file formats including pdfs, word files, images and emails and will flag the sensitive contents from the text which can cause a potential security breach. Additionally it also provides features like providing a overall risk score, monitoring email accounts for sensitive emails and generating special day-wise reports(for emails) for a better all-round analysis.
